Rejection
Therapy

What is it?

Rejection Therapy is all about facing those fears of rejection head-on. At first, everyone is intmidated. But, as you will see, once you purposefully get rejected, and make a game out of it, you will realize that we don't need to feel bad when rejection is thrown into our lives.
Here are some challenges we can do to become the greatest, and fear-less versions of ourselves.

Challenges

Easy

  • Where is ____ location?
  • Do you know the time?
  • Where did you get those shoes?
  • Can I get a compliment?

A Bit Advanced

  • Let's take a selfie together!
  • Push up contest?
  • Rap/ Dance Battle! Right Now!
  • How much is that jacket? I'll buy it.
